About Us:

Welcome to Yarns Of Grace. We are a Christian based shop dedicated to spreading love and warmth through our handmade products. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create Beautiful, Hand knitted products that are not only to those who receive them, but also reflect our faith and commitment to quality. Each piece is knited with care, prayer, and a touch of grace. At Yarns Of Grace we believe in the power of giving. Our products make a perfect gifts for loved ones, or even a treat for yourself. From the scarfs and hats, to blankets and more, you'll find something that suits your style and warms you heart.
